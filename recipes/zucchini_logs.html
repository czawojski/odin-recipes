Zucchini Logs


Description

My friend's wife served zucchini logs as a dinner side, and I was blown away! It's a simple recipe, but it's delicious. I can't take full credit for this one--feel free to steal it yourself!

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchini
  • 1 pack of dry vegetable soup mix
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. slice the zucchini in half lengthwise
  2. use a teaspoon to scoop the seeds out of the center, creating a dish-like cavity
  3. blend the cream cheese, shredded cheese, and soup mix
  4. spoon the cheese mixture into the cavity of each zucchini half
  5. bake on a tray for 20 minutes at 350 degrees
