Guacamole


Description

American guacamole usually features several ingredients and bold spices that create a tasty dip for tortilla chips. Mexican-style guacamole, on the other hand, is often simple because it is used as a taco-topper. My guacamole more closely resembles the Mexican-style, using few ingredients that are blended by hand.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 jalapeno
  • olive oil (approx. 3 T)
  • salt (to taste)
  • hot sauce (to taste)
  • lime juice (to taste)

Steps

  1. peel and slice the avocados
  2. macerate the garlic
  3. chop the jalapeno
  4. fold the first three ingredients together
  5. add lime juice, hot sauce, salt to taste
  6. add olive oil to the desired consistency
