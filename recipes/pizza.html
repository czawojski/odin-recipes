Chanterelle Pizza


Description

Pizza can be anything you want it to be. In my experience, it doesn't get much better than a simple white pizza with foraged chanterelle mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • sauteed chanterelle mushrooms
  • pizza dough
  • olive oil
  • parmesan, ricotta and mozarella cheeses
  • salt (to taste)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • thyme

Steps

  1. spread a thin layer of olive oil on the pizza dough
  2. macerate the garlic and spread it on the dough
  3. add several ounces of cheese
  4. add salt to taste
  5. sprinkle fresh thyme on top of the cheese
  6. bake the pizza at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes
